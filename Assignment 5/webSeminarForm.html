National Workshop on Web Technologies

The Annual Charusat techfest will unfortunately come to an halt day after tomorrow. It's sad to see it all pass away in just a brink of time like the sand slipping away form the palms of one's hands. To wrap things up in this techfest, the National Commitee has arrived on the best way to end this techfest which will include a Workshop on the Web Technologies.
Well don't worry young sports! We'll still have the DJ night as every year!

We are all aware that this Internet is just a bunch of devices which are linked together. Well, a huge bunch! But what is the point of this without having the Websites in which millions of people can hold their business and do million other things.

Well, the commitee believes that almost everyone can and should learn to develop amazing webpages and websites which basically forms the backbone of the internet! So, they have organized a six hour workshop for the begineers as well as the people who has expertise in the field. Any one can participate in this workshop and it is absoloutely free! The people from all the areas including a faculties, non-techinians and most obviously the students can participate in this workshop from any country!

Here are the concrete details for the workshop:
Date: 29/08/2020
Timing: 10:00 to 16:00 IST
Break: 12:00 to 1:00
So, basically the Workshop on Web Technologies is basically divided into three levels of Expertise as below.

Introductory: For those who are unaware of the subject and are eager to learn from scratch.
Begineer: For those who who just got their hands dirty with this subject recently and don't really know much about the subject.
Intermediate: For those who can develop and design webpages, but are yet to aquire industrial knowledge.
Advanced: For those who have a great expertise in the skill.

The Workshop will be guided by the top technitians who know what they are doing.
The meals will be provided by the University itself between the interval.
